# Privacy Policy - Torrey Bible Study App

**Last updated: January 19, 2026**

## Information We Collect

**We collect NO personal information.** The Torrey Bible Study app:

- Does not collect any personal data
- Does not require user accounts or registration
- Does not track user behavior or analytics
- Does not use advertising or marketing services
- Does not access device contacts, location, or other sensitive data

## Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device:

- **Bible Text**: World English Bible stored in app data files
- **Topical Content**: R.A. Torrey's topical organization stored locally
- **App Preferences**: Settings stored only on your device using Android SharedPreferences
- **No Cloud Storage**: No data is transmitted to or stored on external servers

## Permissions

The app requests minimal permissions:

- **Storage Access**: Only for caching app data locally
- **No Network Permissions**: App works completely offline

## Third-Party Services

The app uses only essential Flutter framework components:

- **Flutter Framework**: Google's open-source UI toolkit
- **No Analytics**: No Google Analytics, Firebase, or similar services
- **No Advertisements**: No ad networks or tracking services

## Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages and does not collect any information from children under 13 or any other age group.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be updated in future app versions. The current policy applies to version 1.0.0 and later.
